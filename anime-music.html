Anime & Music

Anime openings and endings are a special part of the viewing experience. These songs are usually written to match the themes and emotions of a series, which is why many fans never skip the opening sequence. Over time, these songs have grown into their own genre within Japanese pop music and are now listened to far beyond the shows they were written for.

J-Pop and rock artists frequently create theme songs for popular anime, and that connection has helped Japanese music reach global audiences.
